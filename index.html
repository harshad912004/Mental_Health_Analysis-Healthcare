üìà Key Insights

  • Doctors in Emergency, Oncology, and Orthopedics report the highest stress levels.
  • Chronic illnesses in patients/doctors strongly correlate with elevated stress.
  • Sleep deprivation significantly impacts mental health in healthcare professionals.
  • Workload levels directly affect job satisfaction and fatigue.
  • Lack of support systems amplifies mental distress among staff.
  • Mid-career doctors (ages 30‚Äì45) are most prone to burnout.
  • Better communication reduces stress and boosts staff satisfaction.
